About Zentech Pharmaceuticals

Zentech Pharma is a quality based pharmaceutical house with highly qualified & experienced team of professionals. The company holds expertise in manufacturing and supplying pharmaceutical & nutraceutical formulations with the aim of providing quality par excellence.

The Pharmaceutical Products, offered by us, are made in compliance with ISO and GMP to gain maximum customer satisfaction. Combining the latest technology along with relentless efforts, we are successfully catering to the diverse needs of the Pharmaceutical industry.

Besides, the company is touching new horizons of success. Under the guidance of MD Mr. Ajay Bansal, we have garnered a top-niche in the domestic market. Our team of competent, experienced professionals is working continuously for the multidimensional growth of the company. We offer safe, efficacious and quality products for improving the health of the community.

Our Mission

Zentech Pharmaceuticals was set up with an aim of providing quality healthcare to one and all. Our mission has been to make quality medicines and pharmaceutical products accessible and affordable to everyone. We relentlessly strive hard to better our range of products which helps us ameliorate the quality of our products along with bringing them within the means and pockets of the common man.

Our Vision

Zentech Pharmaceuticals envisions a world where there is no dearth of quality medicines and healthcare services. In tune with such a grand and noble vision, we at Zentech Pharmaceuticals focus on innovation and strict quality control, both of which help us provide really affordable products which are quality wise at par with the best in the world.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to serve the common man by providing the best of medicines at the most affordable of rates. To us, customer satisfaction is paramount and we manufacture all our products keeping the needs of our valued customers in our minds.
