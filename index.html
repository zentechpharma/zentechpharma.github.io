About Zentech Pharmaceuticals

Zentech Pharma is a quality based pharmaceutical house with highly qualified & experienced team of professionals. The company holds expertise in manufacturing and supplying pharmaceutical & nutraceutical formulations with the aim of providing quality par excellence.
